## Key Results

- **Best Model:** Random Forest — **88.31% accuracy**, ROC-AUC **0.9474**
- **Most important feature:** Insulin (0.31 importance score)
- **Key engineered feature:** Metabolic Risk Score (0.21 importance score)
- All ensemble models exceeded **ROC-AUC 0.93**
